About Zetcoin

Zetcoin (ZTC) is a BEP-20 utility token deployed on the BNB Smart Chain. The project is designed to support digital transactions, decentralized applications, community participation, and future blockchain services. With a fixed maximum supply of 99 million tokens, Zetcoin emphasizes transparent tokenomics, public smart contract verification, and sustainable ecosystem development.
